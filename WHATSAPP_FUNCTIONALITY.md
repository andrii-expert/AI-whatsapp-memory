# WhatsApp Functionality Summary

## Shopping Lists ✅

### Items:
- ✅ **Create shopping item**: `"add milk to shopping list"` or `"add bread to groceries folder"`
- ✅ **Edit shopping item**: `"change milk to 2% milk"` or `"edit bread to whole wheat bread in groceries"`
- ✅ **Delete shopping item**: `"remove eggs from shopping list"` or `"delete milk from groceries"`
- ✅ **Complete/Toggle shopping item**: `"mark milk as bought"` or `"got the bread"`
- ✅ **List shopping items**: `"show my shopping list"` or `"show groceries folder"` (with status filters: open, completed, all)

### Folders:
- ✅ **Create shopping list folder**: `"create a groceries folder"`
- ✅ **Edit shopping list folder**: `"rename groceries to food"`
- ✅ **Delete shopping list folder**: `"delete groceries folder"`
- ✅ **Create shopping list sub-folder**: `"create fruits folder in groceries"`
- ✅ **List shopping list folders**: `"show my shopping list folders"` or `"list folders in groceries"`
- ✅ **Share shopping list folder**: `"share groceries folder with john@example.com"` or `"share groceries with +27123456789"` or `"share groceries with John Doe"`

## Friends ✅

### Friends:
- ✅ **Create friend**: `"add friend John"` or `"create contact Paul - email: paul@example.com"` or `"save friend Mary - phone: +27123456789"`
- ✅ **Update friend**: `"update friend John - change email to john@new.com"` or `"edit contact Paul - change phone to +27987654321"`
- ✅ **Delete friend**: `"delete friend John"` or `"remove contact Paul"`
- ✅ **List friends**: `"show me all friends"` or `"list my friends"` or `"show friends in Work folder"`

### Folders:
- ✅ **Create friend folder**: `"create a Work folder for friends"`
- ✅ **Edit friend folder**: `"rename Work to Business"`
- ✅ **Delete friend folder**: `"delete Work folder"`
- ✅ **List friend folders**: `"show all friend folders"`

## Addresses ✅

- ✅ **Create address**: `"Save address: Paul - street: 123 Main St - city: Cape Town - country: South Africa"` or `"Add address for Home: 456 Oak Ave, Johannesburg"` or `"Save Office location: -33.9249, 18.4241"`
- ✅ **Update address**: `"Update Paul's address - change street to 789 New Road"` or `"Edit Home address - change city to Pretoria"`
- ✅ **Delete address**: `"Delete Paul's address"` or `"Remove Home address"`
- ✅ **Get address**: `"send me Paul's location"` or `"send me pauls address"` or `"send me paul's pin"` (returns address, coordinates, and Google Maps link)
- ✅ **List addresses**: `"list all addresses"` or `"show me all my addresses"`
